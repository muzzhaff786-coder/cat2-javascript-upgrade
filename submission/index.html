About Us - Our Home Kitchen

Welcome to Zahlika Treatz! What started as a shared passion between Munira, Shakila, Halima and Zahra has grown into a busy home-based bakery in Nairobi and Mombasa. We craft custom celebration bakes, specializing in fresh, premium ingredients and beautiful designs tailored exactly for your special events. Order your favorite treats today and experience the joy of homemade goodness. For occasions big or small, we bring sweetness to every celebration. Celebrate your special moments with us! Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or any special occasion, we have the perfect treat for you.

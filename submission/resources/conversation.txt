I interviewed the co-owners of a home-based baking business run by my mother, her two sisters and my sister. They bake custom cakes and desserts, including brownies and cupcakes, operating entirely from home without a physical storefront. 

The primary purpose of the webpage is to showcase high-quality photos of their baked goods alongside a clear, organized price list to attract potential clients. The target audience includes local families, event planners, and individuals celebrating special occasions. 

The owners prefer a cool, inviting colors  like light pink, cream and gold, and a clean visual style. The website must feature a product display gallery with pricing, links to their Instagram and TikTok social media accounts, and a prominent call-to-action directing users to place orders via WhatsApp or direct phone calls.
